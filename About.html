About Mob Control

Learn more about using Mobile Devices as game controllers.

Our Team

We are a small team passionate about creating an accessible and seamless mobile controller experience for PC gaming.

Mob Control is developed with a focus on usability, performance, and flexibility, ensuring that players can enjoy games without the need for expensive hardware.

What is Mob Control?

In the current age of gaming, having access to controllers can be a barrier for players due to the high prices that controllers are being sold for this days.

By using Mob Control, players are transform their mobile devices to controllers, and are able to play games at a low cost alternative as smartphones are the most common electronic devices nowadays.

Our Goal

Our goal is to make gaming more accessible by providing an affordable alternative to game controllers and allowing users to customize their layouts and style to the way they want to play.

Our project focuses on delievering a seamless connection to the desktop application which will hosts the game. A customizable layout for users to edit how they would like to control their game, and to allow multiplayer supports so user are able to play with their friends.
